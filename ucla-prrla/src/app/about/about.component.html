Welcome to PRL

Welcome to the Pacific Rim Library (PRL): your portal to academic libraries across the Pacific Rim. PRL provides access to over 350,000 metadata records of unique and historically important artifacts--including books, audiovisual materials, artworks and more. The collections on PRL are curated by members of the Pacific Rim Research Library Alliance (PRRLA ) to enhance discovery, create a shared resource, and promote the use of PRRLA collections.

About PRL

Our Vision

The Pacific Rim Library (PRL) improves global access to digital scholarly materials with strong research and teaching value for its member communities and beyond.

To learn more about PRL and find out how to get involved visit PRRLA.

Join

PRL aggregates digital content and metadata from participating PRRLA member institutions. If your institution is interested in joining PRRLA and contributing to the Pacific Rim Library, you can read about the membership process online .
